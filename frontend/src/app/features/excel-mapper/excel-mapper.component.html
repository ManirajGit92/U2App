Workflow Notes

  • Upload only `.xlsx` or `.xls` files under 10 MB.
  • Target template is optional. Without it, source columns become the starting target model.
  • Custom expressions are token-based and avoid raw code execution.
  • Excel export sanitizes risky formulas that begin with `=`, `+`, `-`, or `@`.
